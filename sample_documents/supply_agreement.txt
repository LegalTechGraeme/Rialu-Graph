SUPPLY AGREEMENT

This Supply Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between the Supplier and the Buyer, effective as of January 1, 2025.

1. DEFINITIONS

"Supplier" means ABC Manufacturing Ltd, a company incorporated in England and Wales.
"Buyer" means XYZ Retail Group plc.
"Products" means the goods described in Schedule A attached hereto.

2. SUPPLIER OBLIGATIONS

The Supplier shall deliver the Products to the Buyer's designated warehouse within 14 days of receiving a purchase order.
The Supplier must maintain adequate insurance coverage for all Products in transit.
The Supplier shall notify the Buyer within 5 days of any delay in delivery.
The Supplier will provide monthly quality reports to the Buyer.

3. BUYER OBLIGATIONS

The Buyer shall pay all invoices within 30 days of receipt.
The Buyer must provide accurate forecast data to the Supplier on a quarterly basis.
The Buyer will inspect all delivered Products within 10 days of receipt.

4. PAYMENT TERMS

Payment shall be made by bank transfer to the account specified by the Supplier.
If payment is not received within 45 days, the Supplier may charge interest at 2% per month.
The Buyer shall reimburse the Supplier for all reasonable costs incurred in collecting overdue payments.

5. CONDITIONS

If the Supplier fails to deliver Products within the agreed timeframe, the Buyer may cancel the affected purchase order.
Subject to the Buyer's compliance with payment terms, the Supplier shall maintain priority production capacity.
Provided that the Buyer gives 60 days written notice, the Supplier may adjust pricing annually.

6. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement by giving 90 days written notice.
The Supplier may terminate immediately if the Buyer fails to pay any invoice within 60 days.
Upon termination, the Supplier shall deliver all outstanding Products and the Buyer shall pay all outstanding invoices within 30 days.

7. WARRANTIES

The Supplier warrants that all Products shall conform to the specifications in Schedule A.
The Supplier shall indemnify the Buyer against any claims arising from defective Products.
