COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Commercial Lease Agreement is made between the Landlord and the Tenant.

1. PARTIES AND PREMISES

The Landlord is Premier Properties Ltd.
The Tenant is TechStart Innovations Ltd.
The Landlord leases to the Tenant the premises located at 42 Innovation Drive, London EC2A 4NE.

2. TERM AND RENT

The Tenant shall pay monthly rent of £5,000 on the first day of each month.
The initial term of this lease shall be 5 years commencing on March 1, 2025.
The Tenant must provide a security deposit equal to three months rent before taking possession.

3. TENANT OBLIGATIONS

The Tenant shall use the premises solely for office purposes.
The Tenant must maintain the interior of the premises in good condition.
The Tenant shall not make any structural alterations without the Landlord's prior written consent.
The Tenant will comply with all applicable health and safety regulations.
The Tenant shall notify the Landlord within 48 hours of any damage to the premises.

4. LANDLORD OBLIGATIONS

The Landlord shall maintain the structural elements of the building.
The Landlord must provide heating and air conditioning during business hours.
The Landlord will ensure the building complies with all fire safety regulations.

5. CONDITIONS

If the Tenant fails to pay rent within 14 days of the due date, the Landlord may charge a late fee of 5% of the monthly rent.
Subject to the Tenant's compliance with all lease terms, the Landlord shall not unreasonably withhold consent for alterations.
Provided that the Tenant gives 6 months written notice, the Tenant may assign this lease to a related company.

6. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this lease by giving 6 months written notice after the initial term.
The Landlord may terminate immediately if the Tenant fails to pay rent for two consecutive months.
Upon termination, the Tenant shall return the premises in the condition required by this lease, subject to fair wear and tear.

7. INSURANCE

The Tenant shall maintain public liability insurance of at least £2,000,000.
The Landlord must maintain building insurance for the full reinstatement value.
